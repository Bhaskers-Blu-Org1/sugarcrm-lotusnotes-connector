Certificate Accepted

Congratulations! The certificate is accepted and you can now initiate calendar meetings and emails from within the SugarCRM interface. You can now copy and share SugarCRM data including Clients, Opportunities and Contacts through the use of the convenient card list in Notes. Integrating Notes and SugarCRM is intended to save time and make the selling process more efficient.

Accepting the certificate to integrate Notes and SugarCRM is a one-time action. Notes and SugarCRM will remain integrated without any further action on your part.

Please be aware that Firefox affords the capability to delete certificates, so you have the ability to delete this certificate. If you choose to delete it, or accidentally delete it, you need to re-establish the trust by clicking the Accept certificate button in File->Preferences->SugarCRM. (see accompanying Notes SugarCRM Preference dialog).
